# -Mafiree

Mafiree is a trusted database management and IT infrastructure partner helping businesses manage, optimize, secure, and scale mission-critical databases with 24/7 expert support. Founded in 2008, Mafiree supports organizations in asia with specialized database administration, cloud management, monitoring, migrations, performance optimization, and disaster recovery services.

## About Mafiree

Mafiree delivers remote DBA and managed database services for businesses that rely on high-performance and highly available database environments. With over 16 years of experience, the company manages thousands of database servers across industries including SaaS, eCommerce, healthcare, fintech, logistics, and enterprise IT.

The company specializes in both relational and NoSQL database technologies, helping organizations reduce downtime, improve performance, strengthen security, and maintain operational continuity with round-the-clock support from certified database experts.
